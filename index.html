Li-Ching Chen

I'm a PhD student in Computational Precision Health program at UC Berkeley and UCSF, where I am happily advised by Prof. Travis Zack and Prof. Atul Butte. I am interested in incorporating large language models into clinical workflow and aim to integrate subjective patient narratives into predictive modeling. My research currently focuses on the early detection of pancreatic cancer with detailed clinical information extracted from the clinical notes.

Previously, I obtained my bachelor's in Computer Science from National Tsing Hua University, Taiwan, working with Prof. Po-Chi Kuo.
